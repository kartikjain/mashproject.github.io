  • #ICanProtectMyself is to inspire people to share their experiences in the wake of fighting the stigma attached with those who have suffered sexual abuse.

    Share your brave story where you or your any acquaintance fought or witnessed any form of  sexual abuse (stalking, eve-teasing, indecent behavior, molestation, sexual assault).

    Your story might be a lesson for someone else. Get involved!
